Research Interests

I study large-scale real-time on-demand mobility systems (ride-hailing & ride-sharing). My work covers dynamic ride-matching modeling, matching algorithm design, travel demand forecasting, mechanism design, and the coordination of multi-modal intelligent transportation systems — including integration of ground-based ride-hailing with urban air mobility (UAM).
